How to Play

A target word from the Wordle list is shown at the top. Your goal is to guess words that are lexicographically close to it.

Lexicographic distance = how many positions apart two words are when all Wordle words are sorted alphabetically. A distance of 1 means adjacent in the list.

You get 5 guesses. Each guess scores points equal to its distance. Lower total score is better. Distance 0 means you guessed the exact target!

Distance color guide:

0–10 Very close — neighbors in the list
11–50 Close — same neighborhood
51–200 Moderate distance
200+ Far away

Tip: Words that start with the same letter or two are usually closest. Think alphabetically!

Override today's word: add ?seed=test to the URL, or run setSeed("test") in the console. Use setSeed() to clear.
